For the Public

Navigations

How much time do you typically waste in finding a store and then finding the right entrance to it. With this app, you can avail reliable navigational assistant meaning a lot more time for shopping and a lot less for searching.

Parking Assistant

If you’ve driven to a mall, you’d know the struggle that it is to find an empty parking spot and then finding your way into the mall after that. Our interface will allow you to access exactly where there are empty parking spots. It will also guide you to the nearest entry/exit.

Deals of the Day

Get to know which of your favourite stores has deals and offers put up for that day. You don’t even have to go to the stores to know what types of deals are available in what stores. Find it all right here on our app.

Proximity Ads

Want to know what all your options are? Get targeted ads based on where you are in the mall and see all the stores near you.
